## What is it
**ESCAPE_404** is a narrative ASCII game focused on learning programming, real-world consequences, and performance-based progression.

The player does not choose endings from menus â€” the system decides based on actions, acquired knowledge, and decisions made throughout the journey.

## About the Game [+..â€¢â€¢]
- You wake up in a broken system.
  - Nothing is explained.
  - Nothing is guaranteed.

- To escape, you need to understand the system, not just survive in it.
- The game mixes:
  - ASCII exploration
  - Environmental narrative
  - Educational challenges
  - Permanent consequences

Every decision shapes the world, the NPCs, and the ending.

## Main Concepts < / >
- **Emergent narrative:** the story unfolds through the environment and the world's reactions.
- **Integrated learning:** programming challenges are part of the mechanics, not interruptions.
- **Real consequences:** choices affect maps, dialogues, access, and endings.
- **Multiple endings:** automatically determined by player performance.
- **No traditional HUD:** the entire interface is ASCII.
